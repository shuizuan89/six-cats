About the Game

Welcome to the town of Thunderhead. Your job is to deliver milk and orange juice, driving through the desolate streets and fixing your temperamental van. But where has everyone gone?

This game features a unique blend of driving simulation and western horror. With retro PSX-style graphics and immersive sound design, you must uncover the unsettling mystery of what happened in this empty town before the epic, cinematic finale.
