Six Cats Under

A cozy and clever cat-packing puzzle game.

About the Game

Welcome to "Six Cats Under"! This is a charming puzzle game where your goal is to perfectly arrange an assortment of unique and chunky cats to fit within a designated space. It might sound simple, but each cat has its own shape and personality!

Inspired by games like "A Little to the Left" and "Unpacking," this game offers a relaxing yet challenging experience. Rotate the cats, find the perfect spot for each one, and enjoy the satisfaction of a perfect fit. It's the ultimate test of your spatial reasoning and cat-herding skills!
