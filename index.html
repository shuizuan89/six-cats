Kick the Pirate

Test your timing and strength to launch the pirate as far as possible in this hilarious physics-based game!

About The Game

Welcome aboard, captain! You've caught a pesky pirate, and it's time to teach him a lesson. The goal of "Kick the Pirate" is simple and satisfying: use your trusty cannon to launch the pirate across the high seas. Your score is determined by the distance he flies. It's a game of precision, power, and a little bit of luck. Perfect your launch to set new records!

How to Play

The controls are incredibly easy to learn, making it fun for everyone.

  1. Aim: Click or tap the screen once to set the angle of the cannon.
  2. Power: Watch the power meter. Click or tap a second time to set the launch power.
  3. Launch: Watch the pirate fly! Your distance will be recorded at the end.

Key Features

  • Simple One-Click Controls: Easy to pick up and play instantly.
  • Addictive Physics Gameplay: Every launch is different and challenging.
  • Funny Animations & Sound: Enjoy the comical antics of the flying pirate.
  • High Score Tracking: Compete against yourself and aim for the perfect launch.
  • No Installation Needed: Play directly in your browser on any device.
