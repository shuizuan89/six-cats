2048

The classic number-sliding puzzle game.

How to Play

Use your **arrow keys** (or swipe on a touch screen) to move the tiles. All tiles will slide as far as they can in the direction you choose.

When two tiles with the **same number** touch, they **merge into one!** A new tile will appear in a random empty spot after each move. The goal is to create a tile with the number 2048.
