Six Cats Under

A charming and cozy puzzle game about fitting finicky felines into a box.

About The Game

Six Cats Under is a delightful puzzle game crafted with love for the Cozy Spring Jam 2024. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to carefully arrange a quirky group of cats, each with their own unique shape, into a designated space.

It's a game of spatial reasoning that's both relaxing and challenging. With its adorable, low-poly art style and soothing atmosphere, it’s the perfect game to unwind with after a long day. Can you find the perfect spot for every single cat?
